About

My passion for astronomy and physics lead me to pursuit a BSc in Physics & Astronomy in the University of Oslo which I completed in the summer of 2019. In completing the degree I learned a lot of programming through a number of large scale computational projects. My new interest for python and C/C++ projects inspired me to complete my MSc degree in Computational Science: Physics in the summer of 2021. My thesis covered predictions of biophysical signals in the brain, and the thesis PDF and project repo can both be found in the following Github repository.

My current interests include large scale data structure processing, language processing and NLU, and computational neuroscience.
